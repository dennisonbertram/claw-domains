## Overview

Arc is an EVM-compatible Layer-1 blockchain developed by Circle. It's designed as an "Economic OS for the internet" with USDC as the native gas token for transaction fees, enabling predictable transaction costs.

## Official Faucet

### Circle Public Testnet Faucet
- **URL:** [https://faucet.circle.com/](https://faucet.circle.com/)
- **Network:** Select "Arc Testnet" from the dropdown
- **Rate Limit:** Developers can request 20 USDC on testnet every 2 hours per address
- **Amount:** Faucet sends 10 USDC to your wallet

### Circle Support Allowlisting (Alternative)
For higher USDC amounts, you can:
1. Open a support ticket with Circle
2. Include your Arc Testnet wallet address
3. Typical processing time: 24–48 hours
